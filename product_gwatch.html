Products

GWATCH

GWATCH (mkg-001)

Our wireless GPS watches are rugged, small, power efficient and feature two-way voice communications, precise GPS tracking, alerts, small size, and an excellent design that everybody loves. Stay connected to your near and dear ones, even when they are away from you.

Micro SIM Cellular Plans Recommended For GWATCH :

  • Tracking Only: VTS Data Plans (2G GPRS)
  • Tracking and Calling: VTS Data Plan + Calling (2G GPRS)

To learn how to use GWATCH and the GWATCH App please read the Help Page

Features

STANDARD DIGITAL WATCH FUNCTIONS

Time, Date, Battery Power and GSM Signal Strength Display

 

TWO-WAY VOICE COMMUNICATIONS

Ability to make two-way voice calls to preset phone numbers

 

GEO-MARKING

Ability to designate landmarks to alert GWATCH device presence at landmark locations

 

ALERTS

Ability to receive alerts and notifications of all preset alarm conditions on the authorized smart phone

 

GEO FENCING

Ability to designate authorized and restricted zones for GWATCH devices

 

SoS/PANIC ALARM

Ability to send SoS / Panic alarm with a single button press to mobile application

 

MANAGE MULTIPLE WATCHES

Ability to manage multiple GWATCH devices through a single authorized smart phone

 

REAL-TIME LIVE TRACKING

Ability to track GWATCH real-time from authorized smart phones

 

LOGS

Ability to retrieve tracking logs for all GWATCH devices for a 30-days history period

 

LOW BATTERY

Ability to send low battery alert to mobile app

 

MATH GAME

A simple math game for fun

 

CAMERA

Ability to capture photos on the watch and remotely capture on the mobile app

 

PEDOMETER

Ability to track activity of child

 

VOICE CHAT

Ability to send voice message between watch & mobile app

 

FLASH LIGHT

LED flash light

 

WATER SPLASH PROOF

The watch is designed to be water splash proof only and not water immiscible

 

MONITOR MODE

Fast, intuitive and reliable monitoring software, personified to meet all the digital safety challenges

 

DO NOT DISTURB

Inclusion of a DND, essentially turns off the notifications and alarms for a set period
