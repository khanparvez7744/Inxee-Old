Media & Entertainment

Embedded systems foraying into media & entertainment should be capabale of handling an interesting mix of content and communication. These systems demand advanced architectures to handle very large data sizes, data signal processing and data parallelism with soft real-time constraints. Multi-core architectures are very suitable to meet the requirements of such systems.


Inxee provides media & entertainment related services for various formats and codecs under the following areas :

  • Media Servers
  • Media Processing
  • Media Streaming
  • Media DRM
  • Media Networks
  • Human Interactive Media
